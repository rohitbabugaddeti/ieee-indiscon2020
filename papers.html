INDISCON 2020 - CALL FOR PAPERS




Call for Papers

All submissions must be in English only. Authors are invited to submit full paper after converting in PDF format using the IEEE template available at https://www.ieee.org/content/dam/ieee-org/ieee/web/org/conferences/conference-template-a4.docx. Manuscripts must be limited to a maximum of six pages. The conference reserves the right to refrain from reviewing papers that are not in the above format and significantly longer than 6 pages.

Prospective researchers, academicians, authors, students, Industry personnel from India and abroad are kindly invited to submit papers electronically to INDISCON-2020 through Easy Chair. For submission of paper, at least one of the Authors of the paper should be a registered user of Easy Chair. Unregistered authors should first create an account on Easy Chair.

Review process for this conference is DOUBLE BLINDED, which means Authors and Reviewers are restricted from seeing each other's identity. Therefore, the Authors are instructed not to mention the name & affiliation in the manuscript anywhere being submitted for review. Author's name & affiliation details should be included at the time of CAMERA-READY Manuscript submission after paper acceptance.

The maximum no. of words allowed in abstract is 200 words and at least 4 key words are also required. All papers submitted to INDISCON-2020 will be checked for plagiarism according to IEEE guidelines. If found within the limit of plagiarism, it will be peer reviewed and evaluated based on Novelty, Presentation, Correctness, Innovation and Relevance to the conference.

In case of the minor plagiarism, the authors will be asked to rewrite the paper. Such authors should modify in short notice. In other cases, the paper will be rejected. All submitted papers should contain original, unpublished results, experimental or theoretical. The papers submitted should not also be in consideration with any other agency for publication or presentation.

The acceptance of papers along with referees’ comment will be communicated to authors by email. Authors are required to incorporate the comments received in the CAMERA-READY PAPER before final submission.

All the papers accepted and presented in the conference only will be sent to IEEExplore for considering for publication in IEEExplore.

IEEE Policies
The conference will exclude a paper from distribution after the conference (e.g., removal from IEEE Xplore) if the paper is not presented at the conference. Papers are reviewed on the basis that they do not contain plagiarized material and have not been submitted to any other conference at the same time (double submission). These matters are taken very seriously, and IEEE will act against any author who has engaged in either practice.

IEEE Web Page on Plagiarism:
https://www.ieee.org/publications/rights/plagiarism/plagiarism-faq.html
Other useful and updated information such as guidelines and suggestions for presenters will be given later and it will be available on INDISCON-2020 conference website.

The paper should relate to a research activity, case studies or best practices, shedding light on the theory or practice of engineering, technology, or innovation while addressing under the conference theme, “Smart and Sustainable Systems - Decade ahead”.

Topics include (not limited to):
➤ Smart and Sustainable Energy Systems (Renewable Energy Systems, Smart Grid, Energy System Modelling, Power Electronics, Electro fuels, Power-to-Gas, Power-to-heat)

➤ Smart and Sustainable Manufacturing Systems (Information Modeling, Cyber-Physical System (CPS) modeling and architecture, Machine-to-machine communications and multi-layer networks and control, Life cycle engineering, AI for machine tools for prediction and control—from automation to autonomy, Cyber security and control systems for manufacturing and Industrial Internet of Things (IIoT)

➤ Smart and Sustainable Infrastructure (sustainable urban development; urban sustainability; urban sustainability assessment; indicator-based sustainability assessment; sustainability indicators; sustainability indexing model)

➤ Smart and Sustainable Technologies (Algorithms, Cryptography, Data Mining, Embedded Systems, Pattern Recognition, Machine Learning in Circuit Design, ANN, AI Technologies)

➤ Social Networks, Social Media analytics, Sentiment analysis and opining mining, Smart Medical and Health tools.

➤ Signal Processing Techniques, Signal and System modelling, Adaptive signal Processing, Optimization Techniques, Computational Imaging, Deep learning for images and videos, Microwave & Antennas, Emerging applications and systems.
