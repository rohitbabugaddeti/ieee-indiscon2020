INDISCON 2020 - CALL FOR SPECIAL SESSIONS



Call for Proposals for Special Session(s)

INDISCON 2020 organizers cordially invite experts to propose Special Sessions within the scope of the conference. The objective of the special session shall be to have focussed interaction on any of the tracks / topics of the conference with latest developments in the field among the participants that include invited lectures and paper presentations.


The title of the special session, a brief abstract along with the proposer details (Name, affiliation, designation, Short CV) need to be submitted to bhanucvk@gvpce.ac.inbefore 20-March-2020. The proposer of the special session will be informed the acceptance by 23 March 2020. Once the special session is approved, the Special Session Chair will have to publicize the track. In addition, the details of Special Session will be hosted at http://www.indiscon2020.org. Internal reviewing of the manuscript is wholly responsibility of the Special Session Chair and Conference Committee will not interfere.


The manuscripts aimed to the special session, need to be submitted using the same link https://easychair.org/conferences/?conf=indiscon2020 using the Conference Template: https://www.ieee.org/content/dam/ieee-org/ieee/web/org/conferences/conference-template-a4.docx. Conference Plagiarism Information can be seen at: https://www.ieee.org/publications/rights/plagiarism/plagiarism-faq.html


After submission of the manuscript, through easy-chair the corresponding author will have to inform the Paper ID to Prof N. Deepika Rani at deepu_nivas@gvpce.ac.in for consideration into the special session by e-mail.


Technical Program Committee will undertake the review process of these manuscripts like all other manuscripts. Once there are more than six such accepted papers pass through this review process, the Special Session chair will be informed the date and time of the session. At least six delegates need to be registered among these papers. Otherwise the conference organizers will add these accepted papers to suitable other sessions. The special session papers will be added to conference proceedings (accepted and presented papers) and will be submitted to the IEEE Xplore digital library for publication.


Special Session Chair can recommend reviewers after taking their consent. The Technical Program Committee may or may not send these manuscripts to them. A randomized manuscript reviewer mapping will be followed. Special Session Chair will be provided free accommodation, local logistic support, food and travel from his place of work. Only one person will be permitted to be a Special Session Chair, even the proposal originates from a team.
